How to Make Grilled Chicken sandwitch

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below,
but let's go over the basics:

Grilled Chicken Ingredients

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns, split
  • Your favorite toppings, such as lettuce, tomato, mayonnaise, mustard,

Instructions:

  • Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  • In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  • Rub the chicken breasts with the olive oil mixture.
  • Grill the chicken breasts for 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  • Let the chicken breasts rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  • Assemble the sandwiches with your favorite toppings.
